Simulation of variogram estimation
================

## Set-up

  - Population: *meuse* from package **sp**.
  - Variable of interest: *zinc*.
  - Sampling designs: SRS, PPS % *copper*, PWD spread, PWD cluster.
  - Sample size: 60.
  - Estimation method: *wls*, *reml*.
  - Number of replications: 100.
